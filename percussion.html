Biography

Percussionist at heart

I started playing drums in early childhood, then moved to marimba and formal orchestral percussion training. Since then, I have performed with orchestras in Germany and internationally.

I first joined my hometown's orchestras in Kornwestheim and, during my teenage years, became a member of select ensembles such as the Kreisjugend Orchester Ludwigsburg (KJO).

Today, I perform with the Civica Filarmonica di Lugano and with the Landesblasorchester Baden-Wurttemberg (LBO), which became vice world champion at the World Music Contest in Kerkrade in 2017.

Over the years, I have had the opportunity to play with outstanding orchestras, with concerts throughout Germany and international tours across Europe, North America, Africa, the Middle East, and Asia.
